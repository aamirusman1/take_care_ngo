About Us

Take Care is an NGO for poor child education in India. It helps over 10,00,000 children and their families every year. It has projects on education, healthcare, livelihood and women empowerment, in remote villages and slums across different states of India. From the words of Jawaharlal Nehru, children are like buds in the garden that need to be carefully nurtured, as they are the future citizens of tomorrow. Education acts as the wind beneath the wings for children to grow as healthy and prosperous adults in the future.
